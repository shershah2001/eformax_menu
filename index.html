Eformax, a brand of Digital World School Pvt. Ltd., embodies the spirit of maximizing digital transformation. The name Eformax combines: “E” – representing electronic/digital innovation. “Form” – symbolizing structure, configuration, and transformation of processes. “Max” – standing for maximization, enhancement, and growth.
