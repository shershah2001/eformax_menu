1. General

eformax (a unit of Digital World School Pvt. Ltd.) respects the privacy of every individual who visits our website. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information collected and how that information is used.

For the purposes of this policy, the eformax website (www.eformax.com) is referred to as “eformax” and includes the organization and its representatives. eformax will never release information pertaining to its users unless legally required to do so. We have adequate safeguards in place to protect the collected information and to prevent unauthorized disclosure of users' personal information. This policy may be updated from time to time.

2. Personally Identifiable Information

  • eformax does not collect any Personally Identifiable Information about its users unless they voluntarily provide that information. We maintain a log of the IP addresses of all visitors to our website.
  • We collect information from users when they voluntarily fill out a form on our website.
  • This information is treated as personal and kept confidential; it is collected in order to help eformax fulfill your requests. eformax will not sell, share, or rent this information to any third party.

3. Non-Personally Identifiable Information

  • eformax collects Non-Personally Identifiable Information automatically about its users via cookies and various other services to help us improve our services.
  • Information Collected: the type of browser used, IP address, operating system, and the link from which users reach our site—whether through advertisements, search engines, or directories. Additionally, using selected service providers, we collect information regarding a user’s activity on the website and customize certain site functions based on their location.

4. Cookies & Third Party Service Providers

eformax collects Non-Personally Identifiable Information automatically via first-party cookies and third-party cookies.

Cookies are small files that eformax (or one of our service providers) transfers to a user’s computer via their web browser, which collect certain Non-Personal Identification Information. Cookies cannot access or execute programs and cannot contain or deliver viruses.

We use selected third-party service providers to help us optimize our ads across the Internet, such as services offered by Google, including Google Analytics, Google Ads, and others of a similar nature, which require first-party cookies and third-party cookies.

eformax uses this information, unless otherwise stated, for monitoring advertisements and improving marketing efforts. We will not merge Personally Identifiable Information with Non-Personally Identifiable Information, ensuring our users' privacy and safety to the best of our ability.

5. Acceptance Of Policy

By visiting and browsing the eformax website, you, as a user, signify your acceptance of the terms and conditions set forth in this policy. In the event of changes to this policy, your continued usage will be construed as acceptance of those changes.

6. Contact Us

Users may contact eformax with any questions or queries via email at info@eformax.com. We honor all requests to remove information from our databases.
